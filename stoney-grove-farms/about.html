About Stoney Grove Farm

Hello we are Catherine and Ray Browers. We are the owners of Stoney Grove Farm. We handle apple and maple cider syrup, fresh eggs from our free ranged chickens, grass fed all natural beef, and most importantly, our raw and unfiltered honey.
