About Stoney Grove Farm

When we started Stoney Grove Farm, we knew that we wanted to grow and develop healthy food choices for our family. We began by raising grass-fed beef, and over time we developed other natural products including raw honey, maple and apple cider syrups, and free-range eggs. We pride ourselves on the taste and quality of the food and goods we produce.

Thank you for visiting our web site. If you have questions please contact us by phone, text, or email. We also encourage you to create an account so you can share your questions and ideas in our blogging community.

Thank you for your business. We look forward to serving you now and in the future.

Ray and Cathy Browers
