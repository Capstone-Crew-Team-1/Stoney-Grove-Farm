how much honey do bees produce?
One bee colony can produce 60 to 100 pounds of honey per year.
How much water does it take to make one gallon of honey?
The bees use about 8 gallons of water to make 1 gallon of honey
How much beef can you get from a cow?
With a whole cow you would get approximately 440 pounds of beef. It will be approximately 200 pounds of ground beef, and the other 220 pounds are in cuts like steaks, roasts, ribs, brisket, tenderloin, etc
How many flowers must be visited to make one pound of honey?
Two million flowers must be visited
How often do you harvest honey?
In theory, you can harvest honey as soon as you have one or more frames in your super that hold only capped honey. In practice, most beekeepers find it more practical to harvest once a year in the fall or late summer.
